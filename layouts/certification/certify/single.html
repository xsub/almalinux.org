Certification Deep Dive

More Details: Your Path to Certification

Certification with AlmaLinux is a thorough process designed to ensure the highest standards of compatibility and performance. Engage with our GitHub project board to begin your journey, collaborating directly with the Certification SIG and community.

  • Direct Communication: Engage with the Certification SIG and community members on GitHub to discuss certification needs.
  • Submit Results: Upload your test results to the GitHub project board for review.
  • Collaborative Testing: Participate in community-driven testing efforts through GitHub.

Preparing for Success: Pre-Certification Steps

Before testing, complete the necessary pre-certification steps. Ensure all documentation is in order, and understand the collaborative process on GitHub. Our initial review sets the foundation for a successful certification process.

The Certification Journey: Testing to Trust

Our certification testing phase is supported by hands-on QA, covering extensive areas to ensure your hardware is fully vetted for AlmaLinux OS, with collaboration and tracking through GitHub.

Certification Results

Beyond Testing: Certification Labs and Listing

Upon successful completion of the testing phase, your hardware will be featured on the ALOSF Certified Hardware list.

This achievement not only showcases your hardware's compatibility but also grants your organization the privilege of using the term AlmaLinux Certified to highlight this compatibility.
