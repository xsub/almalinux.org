FAQ
What is the purpose of ALOSF Certification SIG?

The ALOSF Certification Special Interest Group (SIG) is dedicated to developing and maintaining the AlmaLinux Hardware Certification Program.

What are the Key goals of the SIG?
  • Standard Development: Defining and refining rigorous hardware certification standards for AlmaLinux OS.
  • Collaborative Testing: Overseeing collective testing efforts to ensure hardware compliance.
  • Quality Assurance: Ensuring high-quality, reliable hardware for AlmaLinux OS.
  • Community Engagement: Actively involving the community and aligning with user and developer needs.
  • Vendor Liaison: Bridging the gap between hardware vendors and the AlmaLinux community.
  • Resource Hub: Serving as a repository of knowledge and resources.
  • Advocacy and Promotion: Promoting AlmaLinux OS adoption through certified hardware.
What is the role of the SIG?

The ALOSF Certification SIG plays a vital role in improving AlmaLinux hardware compatibility and fostering a community-centric ecosystem.
