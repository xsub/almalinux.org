About Certification Process

Introduction: Discover AlmaLinux-Certified Hardware

Explore our comprehensive database of hardware certified for optimum performance with AlmaLinux OS. Our certification ensures reliability and compatibility, giving you the confidence to deploy AlmaLinux in your infrastructure.

Certified Hardware Categories

  • IHV-Facilitated: Hardware certified independently by vendors with their own testing resources.
  • ALOSF-Facilitated (with IHV support): Hardware tested in collaboration between ALOSF and the vendor, harnessing both parties' expertise.
  • ALOSF-Facilitated (without IHV support): Hardware tested solely by ALOSF, often involving newer or less common devices.
  • Community Reports: Community-contributed certification results from hardware tested outside of the official ALOSF labs.
