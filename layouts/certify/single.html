More Details: Your Path to Certification

Certification with AlmaLinux is a thorough process designed to ensure the highest standards of compatibility and performance. Engage with our certification portal to begin your journey, whether it's through a CaseID or direct communication.

  • Direct Communication: Request contact to discuss certification needs or to join the Certification SIG.
  • Submit Results: Upload your test results from the Hardware Certification Suite for review.
  • Ship to ALOSF Labs: Offer your hardware for in-depth testing by our experts.

Preparing for Success: Pre-Certification Steps

Before testing, complete the necessary pre-certification steps. Ensure all documentation is in order, and understand the legal and financial aspects. Our initial review sets the foundation for a successful certification process.

The Certification Journey: Testing to Trust

Our certification testing phase is supported by hands-on QA, covering extensive areas to ensure your hardware is fully vetted for AlmaLinux OS.

Beyond Testing: Certification Labs and Listing

After successful testing, your hardware will be listed on the ALOSF Certified Hardware list. Our labs across the globe reflect our commitment to maintaining high-quality standards.
